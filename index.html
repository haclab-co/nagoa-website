NAGOA is a noble initiative, bringing together auto-garage owners under a professional umbrella body; - independent, nonprofit and apolitical, with a basic objective of bringing sustainable order in the auto-repair industry and also advance vehicle repair trade in Uganda into a more respectable business.

NAGOA has over 180 garage owners all over Uganda and aims to promote self- regulation among members by encouraging formal registration and adherence to national laws, championing road safety, environmental conservation among others.
